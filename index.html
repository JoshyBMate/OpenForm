ABOUT

Cathryn Wright (LLB)
ATTORNEY | NOTARY | CONVEYANCER

Cathy holds an LLB. Law Degree from the University of Johannesburg and is an admitted Attorney, Notary and Conveyancer of the High Court of South Africa. She has experience in the legal profession where she specialised as a Conveyancer and Notary Public. Cathy further broadened her professional expertise by joining an international corporate Shipping Company which amplified her passion for customer centricity and delivering cost-effective solutions for her clients.

Relocating to the Garden Route, Cathy established Cathryn Wright Attorneys. With guiding principles based on building solid trust and enduring relationships with her clients, Cathryn Wright Attorneys provides legal services that place the client at the centre of all that it does.
